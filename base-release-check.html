Cabin Crew Base Release Check

PBA-FM 226 · Issue 1 / 01 AUG 2021 · A350-900

Welcome to your Base Release assessment. This is an important milestone — it marks the moment you transition from training into the operational world as a qualified Thai Airways cabin crew member on the A350-900.

This assessment is not designed to catch you out. It is designed to confirm that you are ready. Every question follows the natural flow of a real flight — from the moment you check in for duty through to blocks-on at your destination. You have studied the manuals, practised the drills, and spent time in the cabin. Now we walk through it together, one phase at a time.

You will be assessed across seven competency areas mapped to the official six-topic form. The standard is clear: demonstrate that you can apply your knowledge to real operational situations. If a topic is unsatisfied, that is not a failure of character — it is simply an area to revisit. You have up to three attempts.

Take a breath. Trust your preparation. Let's begin.
